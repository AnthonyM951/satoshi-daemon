General Investing Information

"Bitcoin Interest Rights" (BIRs) are traded on the GLBSE under the ticker symbol "SATOSHISDAEMON.HORSE".

BIRs have no ownership rights and are denominated in bitcoins (www.bitcoin.org).

The owners of Satoshi's Daemon are putting up their own bitcoins, and have offered to disburse these coins to those willing to accept the risk associated with bitcoins and thoroughbred racing.

Under no circumstances will holders of BIRs be entitled to any US dollar winnings— only the bitcoins pledged before each race by the owners.

Please visit the GLBSE and read the signed contract information.
